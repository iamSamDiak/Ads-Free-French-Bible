<p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-left: 0in; text-align: left; direction: ltr; unicode-bidi: embed;"><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt;">Ford closes the Genk plant while the new generation of </span><strong style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="color: #00b050;">Mondeo, S-Max and Galaxy</span></strong><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt;">are to be launched in 2013.A success could have boosted the site activity.</span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: normal;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman','serif';"> Even if new capacities have been set up in Romania (however lower than in Belgium), the choice of closing Belgian and British plants lets to think that Ford is not confident in a recovery of the European market. As its competitor GM, Ford gives the feeling to have a plan to ebb from a declining European market and turn towards more profitable regions like <strong><span style="color: #00b050;">North America, Brazil, Russia, India and China.</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-left: 0in; text-align: left; direction: ltr; unicode-bidi: embed;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: normal;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman','serif';">With its restructuring process and its product plan, Ford targets a <strong><span style="color: #00b050;">return to profits in Europe</span></strong>as soon as in 2015. The group unveiled a long term operational margin objective of 6 to 8% for Europe in 2015.</span></p>
